About Me

Hi, my name Arlix Sorto and I am 26 years old. I was born in Pasadena, CA, and raised in Grand Island, NE. I received my high school diploma in 2013 from Grand Island Senior High. After graduating from high school, I wasn’t sure If I wanted to go to college, so I decided to work full time at the JBS Swift meatpacking plant. I worked at JBS Swift for six years as a fork operator in the shipping department. While working at JBS Swift, I decided to attend college at the Central Community College in Grand Island and received my associate degree in Information Technology. Now I am a current student at Bellevue University working on a bachelor's degree in web development. My goals in life are graduating from Bellevue University with a bachelor's degree in web development and working for a good company. My favorite things to do in my free time are having family cookouts, playing basketball with friends, and traveling to new places with family.
